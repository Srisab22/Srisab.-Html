Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Description

This is a delicious combination of both buffalo chicken wings and pizza! The best of both worlds combined into one. This recipe includes various different ingridients, and is absolutely worth your time to make it in your home. The taste of the meat combined with various different ingridients makes you wanna have more and more. The pizza can serve up to 3 people, though it mostly depends on the size.

Ingridients

  • 3 cooked chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 (2 ounce) bottle hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle blue cheese salad dressing
  • 1 (16 inch) prepared pizza crust
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Mix chicken, hot sauce, and melted butter in a bowl until well combined.
  3. Place crust onto a rimmed baking sheet or pizza pan. Spread salad dressing over crust, then top with chicken mixture and sprinkle with mozzarella.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 5 to 10 minutes. Let sit a few minutes before slicing.
